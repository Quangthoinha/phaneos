# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Agency owners, founders, and partnership leads at digital, creative, and consulting agencies worldwide. They are evaluating a new revenue stream without hiring an AI team or risking existing client relationships. Context: between client meetings, scanning for credible partners, skeptical of AI buzz, protective of their relationships.

## Product Purpose

phaneosAI is an AI integration partner for agencies. It lets agencies sell AI strategy, workflow automation, custom agents, LLM integration, and AI adoption services to their existing clients. Agencies keep the relationship; phaneosAI delivers the expertise.

Revenue models:
- **Referral**: 12% commission when the agency introduces a lead.
- **Co-selling**: 25% commission when the agency co-sells and co-delivers.

Success looks like: a qualified agency partner submitting the form, downloading partner materials, and booking a discovery call.

## Brand Personality

Deep, trustworthy, easy to work with.

Voice: expert without arrogance; commercial without hustle; calm, direct, and human. The brand should feel like a senior operator you actually want on a client call, not a software vendor pitching dashboards.

Emotional goals: confidence that the agency can add AI services without risk; trust that the relationship stays protected; clarity that the model is simple and fair.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI-SaaS landing pages: gradient-text headlines, floating chatbot widgets, "AI made easy" clichés, big-number metric grids.
- Overpromising automation marketplaces that abstract humans out of the loop.
- Corporate-consulting pages dense with jargon, diagrams, and stock handshakes.
- The 2023-era eyebrow-above-every-section landing page cadence.

## Design Principles

1. **Protect the relationship first.** Every visual and copy choice reinforces that the agency keeps its client relationship.
2. **Expert calm, not AI hype.** Avoid futuristic visual noise; let restraint and precision communicate depth.
3. **One idea per fold.** The landing page is a scroll narrative: hero value, model clarity, process simplicity, service proof, trust guarantee, conversion.
4. **Imagery is the design.** The hero image is a deliberate scene — a person pulling value from light — not a decorative tech illustration.
5. **Global from the start.** All copy, metadata, and voice are in English; design avoids culturally narrow references.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Target: WCAG 2.1 AA across color contrast, focus states, and form labeling.
- Reduced motion: all scroll-driven and entrance animations must respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
- Keyboard navigation: all interactive elements (nav, form, FAQ accordion, CTAs) fully keyboard operable.
- Color independence: no information conveyed by color alone (e.g., commission tiers also labeled by text).
