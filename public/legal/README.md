# phaneosAI Legal Documents

This directory contains the public legal and policy documents for phaneosAI. These files are published as static Markdown and can be linked from the website footer, partner agreements, and client contracts.

## Documents

| File | Purpose |
|---|---|
| [privacy-policy.md](./privacy-policy.md) | GDPR Article 13/14 privacy notice; cookies; data subject rights; Vietnam PDPD disclosures. |
| [terms-of-service.md](./terms-of-service.md) | General terms for website use and service delivery; liability, IP, and dispute resolution. |
| [acceptable-use-policy.md](./acceptable-use-policy.md) | Prohibited conduct, high-risk use cases, and enforcement. |
| [ai-usage-policy.md](./ai-usage-policy.md) | AI transparency, no-training commitment, human oversight, EU AI Act alignment. |
| [partner-program-policy.md](./partner-program-policy.md) | Partner commission tiers, deal registration, relationship protection, and clawbacks. |
| [security-policy.md](./security-policy.md) | Security controls, incident response, access control, and subprocessor security. |
| [data-processing-addendum.md](./data-processing-addendum.md) | Controller/processor roles, SCCs, subprocessor list, breach notification, return/deletion. |

## How to update

1. Open the relevant Markdown file.
2. Update the `Last reviewed:` date.
3. Make the change and save.
4. If the change affects other documents (for example, adding a new subprocessor), update the [data-processing-addendum.md](./data-processing-addendum.md) and any linked references in the [privacy-policy.md](./privacy-policy.md) and [security-policy.md](./security-policy.md).
5. Review annually or whenever a material legal, regulatory, or service change occurs.

## Important notice

These documents are drafted for transparency and operational compliance. They are **not legal advice**. phaneosAI and its partners should consult qualified local counsel before relying on them in regulated jurisdictions.
