- **We do not use your personal data, prompts, or client content to train, fine-tune, or improve any AI model.**
- We use enterprise LLM APIs (OpenAI, Anthropic, and others) under **Zero Data Retention (ZDR)** or equivalent business-tier agreements whenever technically available.
- We apply **human review** before any AI-generated output is delivered to an agency's client.
- For details, see our [AI Usage Policy](./ai-usage-policy.md).

## 4. Cookies and similar technologies

We use only **strictly necessary** first-party cookies and server-side analytics. We do **not** use marketing pixels, third-party ad trackers, or behavioral profiling cookies.

Because we do not store non-essential identifiers on your device, we do not show a cookie-consent banner for analytics under current EU guidance. If we ever add non-essential cookies, we will obtain your prior consent in a clear, easily withdrawable way.

You can disable cookies in your browser at any time. Core site functionality will remain available.

## 6. International transfers

phaneosAI is based in Vietnam. If you are in the EU, UK, or another jurisdiction, your data may be transferred to Vietnam or the United States. We rely on:

- **Vietnam:** our direct operations, protected by Vietnam's Law on Personal Data Protection 91/2025/QH15 and Decree 356/2025/ND-CP.
- **United States:** Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission (2021/914), with additional technical and organizational safeguards for sensitive transfers.
